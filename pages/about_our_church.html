MISSIONS

WE ARE A MISSIONARY MINISTRY.

RURAL MISSIONS

WE SPEND DAYS, MOST TIMES WITH A TEAM OF ABOUT 40 TO 45 PERSONS REACHING OUT TO INDIVIDUALS IN VILLAGES WITH THE GOSPEL OF CHRIST.

WE DISTRIBUTE GOSPEL AND RELIEF MATERIALS, LIKE:

  • BIBLES
  • CLOTHING
  • SHOES
  • FOOD STUFF
  • E.T.C.

EQUIPPING RURAL CHURCHES

WE ALSO HELP IN FURNISHING CHURCHES IN THE VILLAGE WITH EQUIPMENT THAT ARE NOT AFFILIATED TO US, WHERE THE NEED ARISES.

WE HAVE BEEN OPPORTUNED TO PAY FOR A PLOT OF LAND FOR A RURAL CHURCH FOR A PLACE OF WORSHIP.

IN ALL OUR MISSION OUTREACHES WE STRIVE AT STRENGTHENING LOCAL INDIGENOUS CHURCHES IN THE LOCALITY OF OUR MISSION WORK.

WELFARE SCHEME

LOVE GENERALS ASSEMBLY IS INVOLVED IN WELFARE AT VARIOUS LEVELS AND CATEGORIES.

WELFARE IS AN ASPECT OF CHRIST MINISTRY THAT THE CHURCH TAKES VERY SERIOUSLY. THIS IS GEARED TOWARDS ALLEVIATING POVERTY, SUFFERING AND ALSO IMPROVING THE STANDARD OF LIVING OF BRETHREN.
